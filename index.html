Visit Waconia

Enjoy restaurants, wineries and lake activities in this vibrant Minnesota town.

Lunch at Lola's Lakehouse

Grab some fish and chips for lunch at Lola's on Lake Waconia. Be sure to grab a table outside by the water if it's nice.

Rent a boat on Lake Waconia

Head to the marina and rent a boat for the day on Lake Waconia. Cruise around the lake for good fishing spots, or anchor by Coney Island and check out the rope swing.

Wine at the wineries

Enjoy some wine at one of Waconia's three wineries. Schram's is our favorite, but you can't go wrong with any one of them.
